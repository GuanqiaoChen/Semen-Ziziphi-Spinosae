A decisive issue has emerged from the repository itself: the reported “independent” set contains seeds from every one of the same 16 acquisition cubes used for training. More importantly, a standard SNV-preprocessed logistic regression reaches 96.52% on that exact split—305/316 correct versus HS3I-Net’s 306/316. The manuscript’s claimed 14.88-point spatial advantage is therefore largely an under-optimized-baseline artifact, not established evidence of spectral–spatial learning.
